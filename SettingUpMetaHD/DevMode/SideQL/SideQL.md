## Open SideQuest on Your Computer and Connect Headset

---
<img height="70%" src="Images/OpenSQ.png" width="70%"/><br>
* Open SideQuest App on your Computer<br>

---

<img height="70%" src="Images/SQopenFixCon.png" width="70%"/><br>
<br>
* Click on the "Open Setup Instructions" Button, the above screen will open.<br>
* In the Photo above you can see that the headset is not connected.<br>
* Must often it is because your headset is not connected<br>
  * Plug in your headset using your c to c charging cord.<br><br>
  <img height="70%" src="Images/AllowDebug.png" width="70%"/><br>
  * Allow the Debug prompt in your Headset.<br>
  <br> 
---

* This should change all four of your connection status lights to Green.<br><br>
<img height="70%" src="Images/SQGreenCon.png" width="70%"/><br>

* Now you are ready to upload your APK to your headset.<br>

## Uploading your APK/Build and Find it in your Meta Quest 3 Headset.

---
<br>
<img height="80%" src="Images/SQDownloadHere.png" width="80%"/><br>
<br>

* Go back to the home screen.<br>
* You should now see a green circle in the upper left hand corner.<br>
* Click on the square with the downward  arrow on that is circled on the top right side of the screen.<br><br>
  * This will open the file explorer for you to find your APK/Build File.<br>
<br>
  <img height="100%" src="Images/SQFindAPK.png" width="100%"/>
* Locate your APK File <br>
  * Selected your APK <br>
  * Press Open <br>
